Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 100g grated cheese
 - 100g bacon
 - 2 spoons of oil
 - 30g onion
 - 200g spaghetti
 - 1 fistful coarse salt
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. In a dish, combine the eggs and the grated cheese until you reach homogeneous mixture.
 
 2. in a large skillet, add the oil, bacon and onion and let them cook until you want.

 3. boil the water and add the salt. when the water is boiling add the spaghettis and let them cook.

 4. Mix spaghettis and the content of the skillet with the eggs for 2 minutes
 5. Serve and eat!
